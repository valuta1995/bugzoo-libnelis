◆ dout_write()

void dout_write ( uint8_t  data)

Write a byte of data to the entirety of the digital out pins. The least significant bit goes to dout0. The second to least to dout1 and so on, all the way up to dout6 (7th bit).

Parameters
dataThe byte to be written to the dout pins.
